About the Company

THL is a leading developer of complex solutions for e-commerce business worldwide. The company was found in 2016 in Cyprus and gathered high-class specialists in the field of IT development. In three years we implemented remarkable number of complicated projects and significantly increased technological level of our services.

The main feature of THL activity is that the company specializes in building full-fledged infrastructures for e-commerce platforms of any business type (B2B, B2C, C2B, C2C) “on a turn key basis”. THL considers specific e-commerce needs of each industry and deliver e-commerce solutions using innovative designs and modern technology. All operation processes are ensured by the team of professionals and supported through seamless communication channels.
